About Us

Sleep Cool Mattress has gained immense expertise in supplying and trading high-quality foam and duplex mattresses. Our main shops are located in Manjeri and Koodaranji, Kerala, where we are recognized as leading sellers of premium sleep solutions. In addition to retail, we also offer bulk supply of mattresses and beds for shops and institutions, ensuring reliable service and quality. Whether you're looking for single beds for your home or wholesale orders, Sleep Cool is your trusted provider for all your bedding needs.
